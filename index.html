FAQ

Questions, answered.

How accurate are the prayer times?
Prayer times are calculated using your precise GPS location and the Aladhan.com calculation engine. We support 8 different calculation methods including Muslim World League, Karachi, ISNA, and more — letting you match your local mosque's timing.
Does the app work offline?
Yes! Prayer times are cached after the first fetch, so you can use the app offline. Internet is only needed when you change locations or first launch the app.
Is my data private?
100% yes. We don't collect any personal data, don't track users, and don't use analytics. Your location is used only on your device to calculate prayer times. Nothing is sent to any server.
Can I customize Adhan for each prayer?
Yes. Go to Settings → Adhan & Notifications and set Silent, Vibrate, or Loud individually for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha.
Is the app free?
Yes, SalahTime is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscriptions. Built as a gift to the Muslim community.
How does the Qibla compass work?
It uses your iPhone's magnetometer and current GPS location to calculate the precise direction to the Kaaba in Makkah. Hold your phone flat for best accuracy.

Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

SalahTime respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

Data We Use

Location: Used only on your device to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. We never send your location to any server or third party.

Prayer Records: Stored locally on your device only. Not uploaded anywhere.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information, email addresses, names, advertising IDs, or any usage analytics. The app has no accounts, no sign-up, and no tracking.

Third-Party Services

SalahTime uses the Aladhan.com public API to fetch prayer time calculations. Only anonymous location coordinates are sent. No personal data is shared.

Children's Privacy

SalahTime is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
