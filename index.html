Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my location?

Go to Settings tab → Location → either enable Auto GPS or search for your city manually.

Why are my prayer times slightly different?

Different calculation methods produce slightly different times. Go to Settings → Calculation Method to choose the method used by your local mosque.

How do I customize Adhan sound for each prayer?

Go to Settings → Adhan & Notifications. You can set Silent, Vibrate, or Loud individually for each prayer.

Why didn't I receive a notification?

Make sure Notifications are enabled: iPhone Settings → SalahTime → Notifications → Allow Notifications.

How does the Qibla compass work?

The compass uses your iPhone's magnetometer and current location to calculate the direction to Makkah. Hold your phone flat for best accuracy.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information. Your location is used only to calculate prayer times locally.

Does the app work offline?

Prayer times are cached after first fetch, so they work offline. You need internet only when changing location or first launch.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

SalahTime respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

Data We Use

Location: Used only on your device to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. We never send your location to any server or third party.

Prayer Records: Your prayer completion records are stored locally on your device only. They are not uploaded anywhere.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information, email addresses, names, advertising IDs, or any usage analytics. The app has no accounts, no sign-up, and no tracking.

Third-Party Services

SalahTime uses the Aladhan.com public API to fetch prayer time calculations. Only anonymous location coordinates are sent to calculate times. No personal data is shared.

Children's Privacy

SalahTime is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
